My Experience at AkiraChix

Introduction

I reported to school on January the 28th 2023 at 10AM, accompanied by my mother and brother. We had some tea, then an orientation after which parents and guardians had to leave after lunch.

Great Experiences

  1. Making new friends

    2. I got to meet and converse with ladies from different regions and hopefully start long lasting friendships.

  2. Serene environment

    3. The environment around Karen, where AkiraChix is located, is very calm.

  3. Beautiful rooms

    4. The rooms are all en suite and well spaced with balconies that have some amazing views.

  4. Writing my first line of code

    5. Learning all the different tech tools and writing first line of code in diferent languages has been exhilarating.

Challenges

  • Feeling homesick

    • I felt homesick immediately my family left, it was not easy to leave my mother alone.

  • Adapting to a new routine

    • new sleeping and waking up times and overall structure of the day required some getting used to.

  • Not having my phone

    • Not much of a challenge now, but there was a pinch the first two days.

  • Sharing a living space

    • Living with people from different places with different backgrounds can sometomes prove difficult.
